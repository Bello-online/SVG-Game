The Ball Catcher

  • Rules
  • The game immediately begins when you hit the Play button
  • You must move the squares with the "move button" and put the mouse over the square to catch a Ball.
  • The Score is recorded on the score board below
  • Hovering over a square at the right time while the ball passes through it allows the Ball to be captured
  • You can request for music by clicking the music button
  • You may get extra points or no points at all for catching the ball in "Special positions"
  • ** Refresh the page to play again **
